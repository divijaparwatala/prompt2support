RETURN POLICY

Our company offers a comprehensive return policy to ensure customer satisfaction.

RETURN TIMEFRAME:
- Standard items: 30 days from delivery date
- Electronics: 15 days from delivery date
- Defective items: Must be reported within 7 days

RETURN CONDITIONS:
- Items must be in original condition
- Original packaging required
- Receipt or order confirmation needed
- No signs of wear or damage

REFUND PROCESS:
- Refunds processed within 5-7 business days
- Original payment method will be credited
- Shipping costs are non-refundable
- Return shipping paid by customer unless item is defective

EXCHANGES:
- Size exchanges available within 30 days
- Color exchanges subject to availability
- One free exchange per order

For questions about returns, contact our support team at support@company.com or call 1-800-SUPPORT.
